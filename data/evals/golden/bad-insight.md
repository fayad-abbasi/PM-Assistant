# Insight: PM Workflow Improvements Needed

**Source:** interview-2026-03-05-001

---

## Themes

### Theme 1: Users Want Better Tools

Product managers need better tools to do their jobs. The current workflow is inefficient and could be improved. There are several pain points in the daily PM workflow that affect productivity.

### Theme 2: Jira Could Be Improved

Jira is hard to use and takes too long. PMs spend a lot of time in Jira doing things that could be automated. The integration between documents and Jira is not great.

### Theme 3: Communication

Teams need to communicate better about requirements. When things change, people need to know about it. Better communication would help avoid problems.

---

## Pain Points

- Ticket creation takes too long
- Requirements get out of sync
- Existing tools don't work well enough
- The process is manual and tedious
- Teams sometimes work off old information

---

## Confidence: High

This is a well-known problem in the PM community. Everyone agrees that Jira workflows could be better.

---

## Impact: High

Fixing this would help PMs be more productive.

---

## Recommendations

- We should look into improving the workflow
- Consider building some automation
- Maybe look at what other companies are doing
- Think about better integrations
